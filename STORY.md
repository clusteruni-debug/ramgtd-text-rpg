# The Abyss — Full Story Text

> Core question: "What does it take for a person to change?"
> Protagonist: One who knows they need to change but has been running away. Amnesiac. Gender/age unknown.

---

## Prologue — Awakening in the Abandoned Station

### [prologue_01] Awakening
> Darkness. Nothing is visible. A cold, damp floor can be felt. Where is this?

-> Open your eyes

### [prologue_02] Abandoned Station
> Blurry vision gradually focuses. Old concrete walls. Rusty rails. Dusty benches. It looks like an abandoned subway station. No one is here.

-> Search your pockets / Look around

### [prologue_03] Memo Discovery
> You reach into your pocket. A crumpled piece of paper. It looks like your own handwriting, but you have no memory of writing it.

### [prologue_03_memo_first] Memo (Run 1)
**Speaker: Memo**
> '...Don't forget. Why you came here. You must change. Don't run away.'

### [prologue_03_memo_repeat] Memo (Run 2+)
**Speaker: Memo**
> 'You're reading this again, aren't you. I don't know how many times now, but this time, go all the way. You have to pass the Floor of Pain. Watch out for the Collector.'

### [prologue_03b] Area Exploration
> Where the station name sign should be. All the letters have been scratched off. Someone has scratched words into the wall with their fingernails: 'Go down'

### [prologue_04] Guide Discovery
> You fold the memo and put it away. At the far end of the platform, someone is sitting. A ragged figure dangles their legs over the edge, gazing toward the tracks.

-> Approach / Ignore and search for an exit

### [prologue_05_skip] No Exit
**Speaker: The Guide**
> "There's no exit." A voice comes from behind you. When you turn around, the person is somehow standing right next to you. "...At least, not upward."

### [prologue_05] Conversation with the Guide
**Speaker: The Guide**
> "Names are meaningless. Everyone who comes here has lost something." Tired eyes scan you. "You... what did you lose?"

-> I think I've lost my memories / I haven't lost anything / (Run 2+) Are you the Guide?

### [prologue_06] Acknowledging Memory Loss
**Speaker: The Guide**
> "Memories... a common case." He slowly rises. "You need to go down. There's a place called the Abyss. You might find what you lost there. Or..."

### [prologue_06_deny] Denial
**Speaker: The Guide**
> "Nothing lost?" He laughs. Sadly. "People who say that have lost the most. Either way, you need to go down. It's the only option."

### [prologue_06_repeat] Run 2+ Recognition
**Speaker: The Guide**
> "You're back." He doesn't seem surprised. "I gave up counting how many times. But this time, something seems different." A brief light flickers in his eyes.

### [prologue_07] Abyss Explanation
**Speaker: The Guide**
> "The Abyss is structured in layers. Each layer has a question." He points to the end of the tracks. Stairs descend into the darkness. "The first layer is the 'Floor of Pain.' Does pain change people? You'll have to find the answer yourself."

### [prologue_07b] Another Way?
**Speaker: The Guide**
> "Another way?" He shakes his head. "The way up is blocked. You could sit here forever, but how is that different from what you've been running from?"

### [prologue_08] Entering the Abyss
> You descend the stairs. With each step, the light diminishes. Cold air wraps around your skin. A low rumbling can be heard from somewhere. Like a heartbeat.
>
> Deeper, ever deeper.
>
> The Abyss's first layer — the Floor of Pain — unfolds before you.

## B1 — Floor of Pain

> Theme: "Does pain change people?"

### [b1_entrance] Three-Way Path
> A massive space opens up. The ceiling is impossibly high, and a faint red light pulses from somewhere. Three paths lie ahead. Words are carved above each entrance.

-> Corridor of Memories — 'Face your past'
-> Room of Tears — 'See the pain of others'
-> Path of Silence — 'A world without pain'

---

### Route A: Corridor of Memories

#### [corridor_01] Entrance
> A corridor filled with purple mist. Mirror-like objects line the walls, but they don't reflect your image. Instead, blurry scenes — fragments of memories — are playing.

#### [corridor_02] First Mirror
> A scene is visible in the mirror. Your childhood self — huddled in a dark room, crying with knees drawn up. You don't remember what happened, but the emotion is vivid. Loneliness.

-> Face the memory (HP -15, acquire Memory Fragment) / Look away

#### [corridor_03] After Facing
> Pain floods in. Your head feels like it's splitting open. But — a memory fragment remains in your hand. Something cold and transparent like a glass shard. A feeling that you've understood something brushes past.

#### [corridor_03_skip] After Looking Away
> As you look away, the mirror shatters. Shards scatter across the floor, but nothing remains in your hand. Safe, but... a feeling of having missed something.

#### [corridor_04] Second Mirror
> The second mirror. This one is clearer. You see yourself turning your back on someone. They're saying something, but the sound doesn't carry. You're walking away. As if fleeing.

-> Face the memory (HP -15, acquire Memory Fragment) / Look away

#### [corridor_05] After Second Facing
> You ran. You don't know from what, but the fact that you ran is certain. Your chest tightens. That person's expression — you still can't remember. But one more memory fragment remains in your hand.

#### [corridor_06] Last Mirror
> At the end of the corridor stands the last mirror. This mirror is different — it reflects your image. But the you in the mirror isn't smiling. The eyes are hollow.

-> Confront the self in the mirror (combat) / Just pass by

#### [corridor_battle] Shadow Self Battle
> A shadow emerges from the mirror. The same form as you, but — hollow. The Shadow Self attacks!

#### [corridor_victory] Victory
> The shadow scatters. What remains is — strangely, a lightness of heart. The memories aren't yet clear, but just not running away makes something feel different.

---

### Route B: Room of Tears

#### [crying_01] Entrance
> A dark blue space. Water seeps from the walls. No — tears. The entire wall is crying. From deep within, the sound of someone sobbing can be heard.

#### [crying_02] Discovering the Lost One
> You discover a curled-up being. It has no face — the areas where eyes, nose, and mouth should be are smoothly blank. And yet it weeps. With its entire body.

-> Speak to it / Sit quietly beside it / Pass by

#### [crying_03] Dialogue
**Speaker: The Lost One**
> "...I've lost everything. My name. My face. My memories. All that's left is this pain." The faceless being speaks in a trembling voice. "You... have you lost something too?"

-> I've lost my memories too / Can I help?

#### [crying_03b] Sitting Beside
> You say nothing and sit beside it. You stay like that for a long while. The being's crying gradually subsides. Words weren't needed. Just being there was enough.

#### [crying_04] Empathy
**Speaker: The Lost One**
> "I see... we're alike." The trembling calms slightly. "Thank you. I've been here a long time, but this is the first time anyone has stayed by my side."

-> Want to leave together? / I should get going

#### [crying_04_help] Request for Help
**Speaker: The Lost One**
> "Help me? How?" A brief pause. "...If you have a painkiller... even just a moment's respite from the pain..."

-> Give the painkiller (consume item) / I don't have anything... sorry

#### [crying_05_together] Companionship
**Speaker: The Lost One**
> "Can I... leave?" It rises cautiously. "I haven't stood for a long time..." It staggers, but takes steps beside you.

#### [crying_05_helped] Companionship After Painkiller
**Speaker: The Lost One**
> It takes the painkiller. After swallowing — the trembling stops. "...Thank you. Truly, thank you." Despite having no face, it seems to be smiling. "I'll go with you."

### Route C: Path of Silence

#### [silence_01] Entrance
> A blindingly white space. No sound. No footsteps, no breathing, no heartbeat. Perfect silence.

#### [silence_02] Absence of Sensation
> No pain. No sensation at all. At first, it's comfortable. Like lying on cotton. But the further you walk, the greater the unease grows. Something... is missing.

-> (Run 2+) I've been on this path before (hidden route) / Keep walking / Stop and think

#### [silence_03] Unchanging Scenery
> The scenery doesn't change. White walls, white floor, white ceiling. You can't tell if time is passing. Nothing happens here. No pain. No joy. Nothing.

-> Is this heaven? / This is hell

#### [silence_03_think] Pause
> You stop. A life without pain. Is that truly what you wanted? Existing without change, without emotion, without meaning?

-> Without pain, there is no change / Even so, it's better than pain

#### [silence_04_truth] Realization
**Speaker: Voice in the Mirror**
> "You've noticed." A voice comes from somewhere. From the walls? The floor? Or from inside your head? "Pain is proof of change. Without pain, nothing changes."

#### [silence_05_mirror] The Mirror's Promise
**Speaker: Voice in the Mirror**
> "It's not yet time to know. Come down deeper. We'll meet again there." The voice fades. Cracks appear in the white wall. An exit.

#### [silence_hidden] Hidden Branch (Run 2+)
**Speaker: Voice in the Mirror**
> "You're back. Do you remember?" In the middle of the Path of Silence, a mirror stands where there was none before. "No matter how many times you repeat, you always come here. What will you change this time?"

### B1 Boss: The Pain Collector

#### [b1_hub] Return to Central Hall
> You've returned to the Central Hall. You've traveled one of the three paths. Unexplored paths remain — but a new path has opened in the center of the hall. Stairs descending. And someone stands before them.

#### [collector_01] First Encounter
**Speaker: The Pain Collector**
> "Welcome." An elegant voice. A being in a neat black coat smiles. Its eyes — composed of thousands of fragments. Each one looks like a different person's pupil. "Did you enjoy the Floor of Pain?"

#### [collector_02] Conversation
**Speaker: The Pain Collector**
> "Collector... that's right. I collect pain. Discarded pain, ignored pain, forgotten pain." Its fingers caress the air. "Pain is beautiful. It's the only force that changes people."

-> (Empathy flag) Pain isn't the only thing that changes people -> **Dialogue resolution route**
-> I won't give up my pain. I'll fight! -> **Combat route**
-> Take my pain. Just let me pass -> **Submit route**

#### [collector_dialogue_route] Dialogue Resolution Attempt
**Speaker: The Pain Collector**
> The Collector's eyes widen. "...Interesting. You say there's something besides pain? What did you learn from that being?"

-> (Sat beside them) Just being there is enough to change a person
-> (Empathized) Understanding another's pain. That too is change
-> (Helped) That sharing pain makes it smaller

#### [collector_dialogue_02] Persuasion Success
**Speaker: The Pain Collector**
> A long silence. The Collector's thousands of eyes blink. One by one. "...It's been a while. Since someone said that to me." Its coat ripples. "Fine. Pass. But remember — the deeper you go, the harder the questions become."

#### [collector_battle] Combat
> The Collector removes its coat. Beneath it — thousands of pain fragments swirl. "I'll add your pain to my collection."

#### [collector_battle_victory] Combat Victory
> The Collector falls to its knees. The fragments scatter. "...You're strong. But defeating something by force doesn't make the pain disappear." It vanishes, and the path opens.

#### [collector_submit] Submit
**Speaker: The Pain Collector**
> "A wise choice." The Collector extends its hand. Something is pulled from your chest. What was warm, what hurt, what was bittersweet. An empty feeling. "...Go. The path is open."

*-> maxHp reduced to 60, attack -3 (permanent loss)*

#### [b1_clear] B1 Clear
> B1 — the Floor of Pain is behind you.
>
> "What does it take for a person to change?"
>
> You may not have found the answer yet. But you've descended one step deeper.
>
> The Abyss continues.
